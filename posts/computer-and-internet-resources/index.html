Torrenting

  • Get a torrent client like qBittorrent.
  • Subscribe to a VPN service like Mullvad (it's cheaper than streaming services). Most services also have a guide for configuring your torrent client to use them.
  • Before torrenting, use ipleak.net's "Torrent Address detection" to verify that your torrent client isn't leaking information.
